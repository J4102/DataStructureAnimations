Queue

Definition

A queue is a type of data structure in computer science where both ends of the data structure can be modified. By convention, the front of the and back of the queue are known as the head and tail respectively. Upon adding elements - enqueing- the added elements start at the tail. Removing elements - dequeing - will remove the element at the head.
